Introduction

This repository is designed to be your principle study resource while at Elium Academy.

You are currently viewing the source markdown for the gitbook curriculum.

gitbook. The gitbook is meant to guide you through the course, enabling you to know what's coming and to easily find review material by day.

wiki. The wiki is where we will post information that is always helpful, no matter what we're coving that day. You are more than welcome to edit this, or even take it over completely.
